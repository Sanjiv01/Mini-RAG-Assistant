# NorthArc Consulting — Internal Policies (v2.4, effective 2025-01-01)

## Data Retention Policy

Client engagement records, including final deliverables, working files, and email
correspondence, must be retained for **seven (7) years** from the date the engagement
is formally closed. After this period, all such records are to be permanently deleted
from primary storage, backups, and any third-party processors.

Personally identifiable information (PII) collected during an engagement — names,
emails, employment details — is retained only as long as required to deliver the
engagement and is purged within **90 days** of project closure, regardless of the
broader 7-year retention window for non-PII materials.

Audit logs from the practice management system are retained for **18 months** and
are not subject to client-specific deletion requests, as they form part of NorthArc's
own compliance record.

## Expense Reimbursement

Employees may claim reimbursement for reasonable business expenses through the
Concur expense portal. Each claim must include an itemised receipt and a project
code. Claims submitted more than **60 days** after the expense was incurred will be
rejected automatically.

Per-diem caps:

| Location tier | Meals / day | Lodging / night |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 (NYC, SF, London, Tokyo) | $95 | $325 |
| Tier 2 (other major cities) | $70 | $235 |
| Tier 3 (everywhere else) | $50 | $165 |

Alcohol is reimbursable only when part of a client-hosted meal, capped at **$25 per
attendee**. Personal entertainment, in-room movies, and spa services are never
reimbursable.

## Information Security

All work involving client data must be performed on a NorthArc-issued laptop with
**full-disk encryption (FileVault or BitLocker)** enabled and a screen-lock timeout
of no more than **5 minutes**. Use of personal devices for client work — including
viewing client documents on a personal phone — is strictly prohibited.

Credentials must be stored in 1Password. Sharing credentials over email, Slack, or
SMS is a terminable offence. Any suspected credential exposure must be reported to
the Security team via `#sec-incidents` within **2 hours** of discovery.

## Acceptable Use of Generative AI

Employees may use approved generative-AI tools (currently: Claude Enterprise and
ChatGPT Enterprise) for internal drafting, summarisation, and code assistance.
Client-confidential data may **never** be pasted into unapproved AI tools, including
free consumer accounts. Generated outputs that are delivered to a client must be
reviewed by a human author before sending.

## Whistleblower Protection

Reports of misconduct submitted via the EthicsLine portal are confidential and
protected from retaliation. Investigations are completed within **30 business days**.
